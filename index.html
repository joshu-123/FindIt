About FindIt@NSBM

FindIt@NSBM is a student-led initiative designed to bridge the gap between students who lose items and those who find them, complementing the existing official Lost & Found Unit at NSBM Green University.

We understand how frustrating it can be to misplace valuable items like books, ID cards, or even laptops. While the university's Lost & Found Unit does an excellent job, there hasn't been a readily accessible digital platform for students to quickly track, report, or recover their belongings.

Our Purpose:

  • Instant Reporting: Provide a user-friendly web interface for students to report lost or found items instantly.
  • Community Board: Allow users to browse items reported by others, increasing the chances of a quick reunion.
  • Information Sharing: Share crucial information about items that have been submitted to the official NSBM Lost & Found Unit.
  • Awareness: Increase awareness about proper procedures for lost and found items and where to go for assistance within the university.

This platform aims to make the lost and found process more transparent, interactive, and efficient for the entire NSBM community. It reflects NSBM's values of innovation, responsibility, and student empowerment, one found item at a time.

If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to reach out via our Feedback section.
